thick cloud    <br/>

backdrops the passage   <br/>

of ten thousand crows   <br/>
</p>

<p>

sifting through leaves   <br/>

for some unknown thing   <br/>
</p>

</p>

shaking ruby droplets on   <br/>

the tired, matted grass.   <br/>
</p>

-Olivia
